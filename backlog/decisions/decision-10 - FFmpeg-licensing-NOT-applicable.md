## Context

During licensing analysis, FFmpeg/GPL licensing implications were considered as a potential concern for the project.

## Decision

This bridge has no audio/video encoding/decoding functionality. FFmpeg/GPL licensing considerations do not apply. The project is purely a weather data HTTP-to-MQTT bridge.

## Consequences

No GPL compliance burden from FFmpeg. Clear and straightforward licensing path. No additional third-party attribution needed for multimedia codecs.
